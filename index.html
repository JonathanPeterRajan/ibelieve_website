Our Prayer Meeting

 

ibelieve hosts a prayer meeting to intercede for Scotland each Saturday at 6pm (UK time).

This week will be our eighth prayer meeting.  Each week we see God connecting His family from across the globe to pray.  It is very humbling to see the passion of people from Dubai, Bahrain and India stand in the gap and pray for Scotland.  We would love you to join with us.  On this Easter weekend we will be sharing communion too.

​The zoom ID and password for the prayer meeting is displayed on the image to the right.  If you are a follower of Jesus and have a heart for revival (no matter where in the world you are) please join us as we come in agreement for Scotland and the nations of the world.  Contact me if you would like more information.
